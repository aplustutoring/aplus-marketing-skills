## What this week's anchor is arguing and who it is for

The blog argues that the spring before a summer break is the right time to lock a charter LEA's Tier 3 cohort, not the fall after the gap has widened. With spring MAP windows closing this week and the cohort identifiable from data already in hand, charter executive directors, special programs coordinators, and federal program coordinators have a 21-day planning window that closes when summer staffing decisions lock in early June. The piece walks the operational protocol: pull spring data by May 23, cross-reference IEP and EL status by May 25, lock the cohort by May 28, confirm same-tutor staffing and 18-24 hour dosage by June 5, runway starts July 8. The piece doubles as Danielle's sales asset through July and as a permanent SEO asset for the "charter school summer intervention program Tier 3" query cluster.
