**Target word count:** 100 (90-110)

---

Charter directors will wait for the November designation letter. They always do.

Then they will scramble to find a vendor in December, sign a contract in January, onboard students in February, and submit a CSI plan in March with four weeks of data behind it.

The schools that have a real program running by August submit a plan in March with seven months of data behind it.

It is the same plan template. The difference is whether the school has results to point to or a promise to deliver.

The federal accountability calendar does not slow down for anybody.
