CDE issued the 2025-26 Aligned Release Letter on May 5. The 2026 California School Dashboard arrives this fall and determines CSI and ATSI designations for the 2026-2029 federal accountability cycle.

Two things California charter LEAs should know.

First, the two-Dashboard-year rule. A charter is evaluated against the same performance thresholds as a district but must meet them across both the 2025 and 2024 Dashboards. A school that just cleared its 2025 reading can still be designated based on the combined two-year pattern. The implication is operational: a charter executive director cannot look at the 2025 Dashboard alone and conclude the school is safe.

Second, the planning timeline. Designation letters arrive within weeks of the Dashboard release. The intervention plan must be developed in partnership with educational partners and beginning implementation by the second semester. A charter LEA that starts vendor procurement in October is already behind the calendar.

The charter LEAs that come into November with a Tier 3 program already running are not scrambling to "develop a CSI plan." They are the plan.
