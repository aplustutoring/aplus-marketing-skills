### Scene direction

A charter school administrator (mid-40s, professional but not corporate; could read as a charter executive director or special programs coordinator) at a real office desk reviewing a paper expenditure report. A wall calendar in the background shows September 2026 with the 30th circled. Light streams in from a window beside the desk. ### Why Canva over image gen for pull quotes

Text-on-image graphics live or die by typography precision. Image generation produces unreliable typography (garbled letters, wrong fonts, inconsistent kerning). Canva produces clean type every time. All three pull quote graphics should be Canva-executed.
