# A+ Tutoring B2C Brand Kit

## When to apply

Apply this brand kit whenever creating:
- Facebook posts (A+ Tutoring page)
- Instagram posts and stories (@aplustutoring)
- Parent-facing emails (transactional, newsletters, milestone emails)
- Family newsletters
- Student spotlight content
- Content for wetutorathome.com
- Marketing materials targeted at parents or families
- Visual graphics and Canva assets for B2C audiences

Do NOT apply this brand kit for:
- LinkedIn content (use aplus-b2b-brand-kit)
- School admin pitch materials (use aplus-b2b-brand-kit)
- Case studies for charter directors (use aplus-b2b-brand-kit)
- Sales collateral for B2B audiences

## Brand colors

### Primary
- **A+ Orange:** `#EF5829`
- Use for: headlines, primary CTAs, brand emphasis, hero sections, button backgrounds
- This is the lead color in B2C. Warm, energetic, approachable.

### Secondary
- **A+ Navy:** `#1A3A52`
- Use for: body headings, footer backgrounds, secondary CTAs, trust signals
- Provides credibility balance to the warm primary

### Accent
- **A+ Gold:** `#F4A261`
- Use for: highlights, win moments, achievement callouts, milestone badges
- Use sparingly — accents should feel earned, not decorative

### Neutrals
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` — primary background
- **Warm Off-White:** `#FAF7F2` — alternate background, content cards
- **Charcoal:** `#2E2E2E` — primary body text
- **Light Gray:** `#E8E8E8` — dividers, borders, subtle backgrounds

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font:** Inter (Google Font, free)
- **Weight:** Bold (700) for H1, Semibold (600) for H2/H3
- **Size guide:**
  - H1: 32-40pt
  - H2: 24-28pt
  - H3: 18-22pt

### Body
- **Font:** Inter (Google Font, free)
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Size guide:**
  - Body: 16pt
  - Small: 14pt
  - Caption: 12pt

### Why one font?
Inter handles both headline and body well. Reduces decision-making, ensures consistency, works across all platforms (Canva, HubSpot, LinkedIn, Instagram).

## Color combinations (do this)

✅ **Orange headline on white background** — primary brand expression
✅ **Navy headline on warm off-white background** — content cards
✅ **White headline on navy background** — footers, secondary CTAs
✅ **Gold accent on white or warm off-white** — achievement callouts, quote highlights
✅ **Charcoal body text on white or warm off-white** — all body content

## Color combinations (never do this)

❌ Orange on navy (vibrating, unreadable)
❌ Gold on white (too low contrast)
❌ Charcoal on charcoal variations (no hierarchy)
❌ More than 3 colors in one composition (visual chaos)

## Image style

### Use
- Real photos of students with tutors (when available, with consent)
- Warm, naturally-lit photography
- Diverse families and students reflecting actual A+ client base
- Bright, hopeful imagery — kids engaged in learning, not stressed

### Avoid
- Stock photos of overly happy generic families that don't match A+ demographics
- Chalkboards (cliché tutoring imagery)
- Apple emojis (overused tutoring trope)
- Comic Sans or any decorative fonts
- Gradients with more than 2 colors
- Stock photos that obviously look like stock photos

## Voice cues for B2C content

When this skill is active, content should:
- Address parents directly ("your child," "your family," "you")
- Use warm, parent-relatable language ("we get it," "we've been there")
- Lead with student outcomes (confidence, grades, engagement)
- Avoid educational jargon (LTEL, MTSS, Title III) — that's B2B language
- Include specific testimonial-style language when possible
- Speak to the parent's hopes, not just the student's needs

## Tone descriptors

- Warm
- Approachable
- Hopeful
- Professional but human
- Parent-relatable
- Confident without being preachy

## Banned words and phrases

- "All students" (Roman/Danielle voice rule — applies here too)
- "Empower" / "unlock potential" / "transform learning" (corporate fluff)
- "Game-changer" / "best-in-class" / "industry-leading"
- Profanity
- AI-detection vocabulary: "leverage," "delve," "harness," "foster," "fundamentally," "streamline"

## Logo usage

- **Logo file:** `Hi-Res Logo (320 × 230 px)` (currently hosted on wetutorathome.com)
- Always include logo in headers, footers, and key brand touchpoints
- Maintain minimum 20px clearspace around logo
- Do not stretch, recolor, or modify logo
